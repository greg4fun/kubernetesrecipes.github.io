## Severity levels

- **Critical** — Breaks indexing, security, or revenue-critical paths. Fix immediately.
- **High** — Significant SEO or reliability impact. Schedule promptly.
- **Medium** — Quality or best-practice gap. Plan into normal work.
- **Low** — Minor or cosmetic. Address opportunistically.
- **Info** — Change-detection signal — no action required unless unexpected.

## Ownership

- **engineering** — Code, server, redirect, and infrastructure fixes.
- **content** — Copy, metadata, structured data, and editorial fixes.
- **design** — Layout, performance, accessibility, and UX fixes.

## How to read this legend

- **Key** — the exact `issue_type` value used in the export.
- **Meaning** — what the check detects and why it matters.
- **Constraint** — the condition or threshold that triggers the issue.
- **How to solve** — recommended remediation.

Checks are grouped by category in dashboard display order.
